  • Where can I enter for a chance to win a free laundry service?
    • Stop by the “Strike Out Stains” booth, located in the Fan Fun Zone under the Sponsor Tent at the Little League® World Series
  • How do you select the 50 winners?
    • Winner selection is random. 50 winners are selected by 7pm EST each day starting August 16th – August 23rd
  • How will I know if I’m a winner?
    • You will receive a congratulations email from the #SOSLaundry Experts no later than 7pm EST for pick-up the following morning
  • If I am a winner, how do I confirm that I want to participate?
    • If you receive an email from us, you will be instructed to call the SOS Hotline (number provided in email) to official confirm your participation in the free laundry service. You must call no later than 7am EST the morning of pick-up
  • What if I don’t win on the day I sign up?
    • If you don’t win the same day that you sign up, you still have a chance to win a free laundry service. You will be entered to win into the next day’s random selection. You only need to enter once, so check your email each day to see if you are a winner. Winners will be selected starting August 16th – August 23rd
  • What do I get for entering?
    • (1) SOS plastic “dirty” laundry bag and (1) #SOSLaundry tag
  • What if I win, and don’t want to participate anymore?
    • If you do not wish to participate in the free laundry service, but received a congratulations email, do not call the SOS Hotline to confirm your participation
    • You can also email us at strikeoutstains@gmail.com with any questions
  • What if I win, and don’t have enough laundry yet? Can I request a later date?
    • Unfortunately, no you can not request a later date, but the #SOSLaundry Experts are happy to wash whatever dirty laundry you have
  • How much clothes can I submit?
    • As many items that fit into the SOS dirty laundry bag. Please note, the bag must be tied in a knot with the #SOSLaundry tag attached
  • Can my laundry be sorted?
    • No, whatever items are in the bag will be washed together
  • What will the #SOSLaundry Experts do?
    • Our #SOSLaundry Experts will pick-up, wash, dry, fold and drop-off your laundry bag within 24 hours
  • Are there any Terms & Conditions?
    • See our Rules & Regulations
  • How do I provide feedback?
    • A Satisfaction Survey will be emailed to you. Please fill this out to let us know how we did!
